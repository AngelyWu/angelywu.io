Process and solutions

We could make the user experience with the project the best it can be. The working process will cover the following four phases.

Research

The first step and core of the design thinking process is to empathize with your users. User research is one of the best ways to do that.

Design

Take complex information and turn it into user-friendly UI design solutions including user flows, wireframes, prototypes and interaction design work.

Development

With the preparation of planning and UI design, we will combine and translate the design concepts and ideas into reality that is ready for development.

Testing

User testing is the process of collecting feedback and data to evaluate a product and feature. It’s an excellent way to gain valuable insights into what works and what doesn’t.

IOS app design

Each IOS project contains a set of design thinking and process. There are many things to consider when designing for mobile. This rough framework outlines my process of designing mobile app experiences:

1. Communicate with client or PM to collect relevant information and achieve goals and user definitions for app.
2. Research and gather the present state
3. Create, evaluate and iterate deliverables like user-flow, wireframes, mockups and prototypes.
4. Design revisions and final approval.

The conceptual approach/UX strategy and reasoning behind it, is present during the whole process.
